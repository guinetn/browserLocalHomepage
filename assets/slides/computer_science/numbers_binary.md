### Binary (base 2)

Bits 
    binary numbers: the 0s and 1s common to digital computers
    A bit is a binary digit - 0 or 1, true or false, on or off. 

Bytes
    Eight bits is a byte, which is the basic unit of information that computers work with.

Nibble
    4 bits

|  binary | decimal| 2^x |
|----|----|----|
|00000001| 1|    0|
|00000010| 2|    1|
|00000100| 4|    2|
|00001000| 8|    3|
|00010000| 16|   4|
|00100000| 32|   5|
|01000000| 64|   6|
|10000000| 128|  7|
|11111111| 255|  8|
| 8 bits = 1 byte|||
