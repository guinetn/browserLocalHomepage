### Hexadecimal (base 16)

Each group of four bits makes up a hexadecimal, or base 16, number
1111 in binary = 15 in decimal = F in hexadecimal 

|Binary|Hexa|Decimal|
|---|---|---|
|0000| 0 | 0 |
|0001| 1 | 1 |
|0010| 2 | 2 |
|0011| 3 | 3 |
|0100| 4 | 4 |
|0101| 5 | 5 |
|0110| 6 | 6 |
|0111| 7 | 7 |
|1000| 8 | 8 |
|1001| 9 | 9 |
|1010| A | 10 |
|1011| B | 11 |
|1100| C | 12 |
|1101| D | 13 |
|1110| E | 14 |
|1111| F | 15 |

Prefixes
* 0x hex number prefix
* 0b binary number prefix
