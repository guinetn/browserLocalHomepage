## Solving process
* Identify problem: what's going on?
* Gathering data: what do we know, ask
* Analyze data: root causes?
* Ask for more information
* Generate solutions: what could be done?
* Select the best solution: explain why
* Plan implementation
* Implements & test: probelm solved?
* Continuously improve
