## augmented reality

- https://www.red-gate.com/simple-talk/dotnet/c-programming/unity-and-augmented-reality/

Vuforia is an SDK for creating AR applications on mobile devices. It allows the application to detect real-world objects. The developer creates a database of “image targets” that will be fed into the Unity app. These image targets are, as you may have guessed, 2D images of what to look for in the real world.
