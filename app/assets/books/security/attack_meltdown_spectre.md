## meltdown-spectre

Vulnerabilitie caused by the way processors manage spéculative exécution (processor infer next code to execute and 'prepare it'). Conception default
Discovered by 'Project Zero' Google Team

Fix: replace processor and/or OS (slowdown perf 5-30%)

- Meltdown
  Intel, Apple processors
- Spectre 
    Intel, Apple, ARM, AMD processors
    More difficult to exploit
    More difficult to mitigate
