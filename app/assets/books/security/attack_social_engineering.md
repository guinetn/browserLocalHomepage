# ATTACK BY SOCIAL ENGINEERING

Manipulate people/organisations for them to disclose sensitive information (connexion, paiement)
- pretend to be a delivery man...
- pretend to be a the company president and urgently need...
- Email to download a film, winned a price
- Loose usb keys on the company parking with written "confidential"

- Best defense against social engineering attacks is common sense combined with up-to-date knowledge of the most common social engineering attacks.
- Don't click links on emails
- Be wary of any suspicious message
