### UDP - User Datagram Protocol
A protocol that sends independent packets of data, called datagrams, from one computer to another with no guarantees about arrival. 
"NOT CONNECTED" BASED: send asap, no acquitment
Example: time request, ping, streaming (tv, radio, VoIP)

Use datagram: 
| Port Source | Port Destination | Longueur totale | Checksum | Données à envoyer |
