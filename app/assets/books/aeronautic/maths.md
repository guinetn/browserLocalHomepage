## Maths for aeronautic

![](assets/books/aeronautic/assets/lat-long.png)
https://medium.com/swlh/calculating-the-distance-between-two-points-on-earth-bac5cd50c840


Latitude φ: (-90°<φ≤90°). the angle between the equatorial plane and the line passing through the center of the earth and the point. Positive values are north of the equatorial plane, negative ones south.

Longitude λ (-180°<φ≤180°): the angle between the prime meridian (Greenwich) plane and the line passing through the center of the earth and the point.
