# BRANCHES

## 1. Analytical chemistry
Determine what matter is and how much if it exists: quality of water/food, provide measurements and documentation to trade

## 2. Organic chemistry
Study of the structure, properties and reactions of compounds and materials containing carbon atoms
Life, plastics, chemical, food, paints, explosive, comsmetics...create new molecules and explore properties

## 3. Physical chemistry
Study of how matter behave on molecular and atomic level and how chemical reaction occurs

## 4. Biological chemistry
Study of structure, composition and checmical reactions of substances in living systems

## 5. Inorganic chemistry
Study of properties and behavior of inorganic compounds (without carbon atoms):
metals, minerals, organometallic compounds: pigments, coatings, medecines, fuels
